Presidential Election-2019 in Ukraine

(Model for «Poll of polls»)

We took all reliable polls and plotted aggregated voter's intentions on interactive chart. The values are percentage of voters who are going to vote.   Last updated: March 4, 2019

Click on bars with name of candidate to add or remove corresponding charts.

Click on bars with name of candidate to add or remove corresponding charts.

Each line on chart is an evolution of median for support rate for some candidate. We calculated it with a help of probabilistic model*.

Please, take into account that support level for some candidate in form of single number is oversimplification. We've got a whole distribution for level of support, so besides median you should look to entire range of values for specific level.

So please, pay an attention to the band of color around each line ― this is the range of values for level of support for specific candidate. So, with a big probability (0.95) the level of support is within such range.

Please, remember: this is NOT an election forecast! This is just aggregated levels of support for candidates due to number of different polls.
